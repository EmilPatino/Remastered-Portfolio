About Me

Dedicated to Innovation & the Democratization of Technology

I am a dedicated product management professional who believes in hardwork, integrity, humility, and the ever-giving value of continous learning. I aim to dedicate my life to leading innovative, technological solutions that further the human condition. I thrive in entrepreneurial environments that allow me to infuse my creativity, love of learning, and compassion for others into my work.

My love of learning has led me to expand my aptitude for both technical and business subject matter empowering me to become a versatile leader on multidisciplinary teams. My career as a consultant has allowed me to deliver value across a wide portfolio of technical projects. I have been fortunate to work for the Department of Justice's 9/11 Victim Compensation Fund, the Transportation Security Administration, the Department of Homeland Security's Office of the Chief Technology Officer, and Capital One's Delivery Experience division.

I graduated Magna Cum Laude from Georgetown University as a dual major of Art History and Russian Studies. My certifications include: Project Management Professional (PMP), AWS Solution Architect Associate, AWS General Cloud Practitioner, Certified Scrum Master (CSM), and Certified Associate of Project Management (CAPM).
